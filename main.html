About Residence Hotel

Residence Hotel Manila, a Forbes 5-star destination for six consecutive years in the Philippines and a Forbes VERIFIED Responsible Hospitality integrated resort, seamlessly blends unmatched hospitality, gaming, and entertainment across 30 breathtaking hectares.

Our hotel is designed with your comfort in mind, offering a range of amenities to make your stay enjoyable.

Dining Area

With over 40 dining choices, Residence Hotel Manila serves up a diverse tapestry of beloved flavors from across the globe. From meticulously prepared Japanese sushi and handmade Italian pasta to intricate Shanghainese specialties, each dish showcases premium ingredients and a deep respect for culinary heritage.

Whether you're enjoying a leisurely breakfast or a romantic dinner, our dining area offers a warm and inviting atmosphere, complemented by exceptional service.

Swimming Pool

Residence Hotel Manila features multiple luxurious swimming pools, including the outdoor Coral Wing Pool with scenic views of Manila Bay and the indoor, glass-domed Cove Manila Beach Club.

Whether you want to take a refreshing dip or lounge by the water, our pool area offers the perfect escape.
